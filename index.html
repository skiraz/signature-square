Our story

Coffee house by day, living room by night.

Signature Square was built for people who linger. By morning the courtyard fills with sunlight, cold brews, and quiet conversation. As the light drops, the same tables hold shisha smoke, plated dinners, and the kind of nights that turn into next week's plans. Every corner — from the brass-lit entrance to the palm-shaded patio — was designed to feel like somewhere you already belong.
